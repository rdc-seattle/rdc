Who we are

A donation to Relay Dance Collective goes toward compensating the dancers (who perform in at least two pieces) each season, covering our operating costs (venue, costumes, technical needs) and our community outreach programs including both a touring performance and fundraising to provide other opportunities for youth to experience dance.


$10 -- An hour of studio time
$25 -- A costume
$50 -- Maintain our website
$100 -- Help compensate a dancer
$300 -- Rent a stage

Many of our dancers take time off of their earning hours to volunteer their time in our social outreach performances.
