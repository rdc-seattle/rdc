Support Relay Dance Collective

Help Us Bring Dance to Seattle

Community outreach is the heart of RDC. We are committed to expanding the audience and participants of dance through the presentation of diverse works and partnerships with other community organizations.

How Your Donations Help

Many of our dancers take time off of their earning hours to volunteer their time in our social outreach performances. A donation to Relay Dance Collective goes toward compensating the dancers (who perform in at least two pieces) each season, covering our operating costs (venue, costumes, technical needs), and our community outreach programs. This includes both a touring performance and fundraising to provide other opportunities for youth to experience dance.


What Your donations Provide

  • $10  --   An hour of studio time
  • $25  --   A costume for a dancer
  • $50  --   Our website's maintenance costs
  • $100  --  Compensation for a dancer
  • $300  --  Stage rental
