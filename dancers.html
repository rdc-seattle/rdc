Meet Our Collaborators

Over the past two years we have been lucky to work with some of the most talented and giving dancers we have ever met. They, along with our amazing technical volunteers and costume designers, pour themselves into our performances and community work.

Each season is an amazing thing to watch unfold as regular contributors and new members alike work together to create something new.
