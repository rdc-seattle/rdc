Meet RDC's Performers

Relay Dance Collective consists of four Seattle based co-founders that focus on providing Seattle audiences with a diverse set of work. Each co-founder provides an individual artistic perspective. Dive deeper and get to know each co-founder.

Rehearsal Info & Community Classes

Relay Dance Collective rehearses with various choregraphers, dancers and musicians to perfect dancing techniques and self-expression. Relay also provides community dance classes throughout the year in the greater Seattle area. Find the perfect class for you!

Past performances

Relay Dance Collective performs an annual spring showcase that features fresh choreography, live music, and collaborators including dancers, musicians and artists. Relay also performs several smaller shows throughout the year. Check back seasonally for updated schedules.
